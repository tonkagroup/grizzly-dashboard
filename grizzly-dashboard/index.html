How October 2025 filled in — nights on books by snapshot date

October bookings don't appear until late June 2025. All 1,225 nights booked after Mar 27.
Why October is separate: Oct is a shoulder month (season closes Oct 15). In 2025, zero Oct nights were on books at Mar 27 — all 1,225 booked after that date, mostly in-season. Including Oct in the main model would distort the late fill calculation and make YoY comparisons misleading since 2026 Oct bookings are still early. Watch this tab from July onward when Oct 2026 bookings start to accumulate meaningfully.
